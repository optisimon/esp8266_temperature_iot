  • A reset button can be connected between RST and GND.
  • A status led can be connected directly between L+ and L-.
  • NTC temperature sensors (10KOhm at 25 degree C, and beta of 3950) can be connected between any NTC terminal and nearby GND terminal.
  • Up to six one wire temperature sensors (DS18B20) can be connected to OW_+ (3.3V), OW_DQ (data), and GND.
Currently, up to six sensors can be monitored simultaneously, and those can be an arbitrary mix of NTC and one wire temperature sensors.
